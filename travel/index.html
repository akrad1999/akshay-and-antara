Travel
FAQ

Do I need a visa for Thailand?

Guests traveling with ordinary passports from the United States, United Kingdom, and Singapore are currently eligible for visa-exempt short tourist stays. Please check the official Thai visa guidelines to confirm your eligibility before travel. Check official visa guidance ↗

What is the Thailand Digital Arrival Card?

All non-Thai nationals are required to complete the free Thailand Digital Arrival Card online within three days before arriving in Thailand. Open the official TDAC ↗

How long should my passport be valid?

Thai travel guidelines recommend that your passport have at least six months of validity remaining from your date of arrival. Please travel with the same passport used for your TDAC submission. Review embassy guidance ↗

My flight arrives on March 22. What should I do?

Please contact Antara or Akshay directly to coordinate your arrival. Scheduled wedding shuttle transportation will be available on March 21 only.

Do I need cash in Thailand?

All wedding-hosted meals and events at the resort are included. We recommend carrying some Thai baht for night markets, local vendors, excursions, and any personal purchases outside the resort.

When will shuttle pickup details be shared?

Your shuttle schedule, pickup time, and meeting location will be shared once we have received your flight information.

What food options will be served throughout the event?

The resort will provide breakfast, lunch, dinner, tea, and snacks throughout the stay, with a variety of vegetarian and non-vegetarian buffet options available. In accordance with our wedding menu, beef and pork will not be served during the resort stay.
